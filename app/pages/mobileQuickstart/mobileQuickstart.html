WebTrader for Mobile

Access WebTrader for Mobile by navigating to the following link on your mobile device: WebTrader for Mobile

Login

Enter the credentials provided to you in the WebTrader demo email. Tap Login at the bottom of the screen to continue.

Market Watch

You will be taken directly to the Market Watch screen upon logging in. The Market Watch component displays real time quotes for all currency pairs that the you have access to trade. From here, you can tap on any currency pair to display it in the charting module.

Chart Settings

You can adjust the Chart Style, Period, and Study by tapping the icon in the top right corner of the application. After each selection, the chart will refresh.

This is the EURUSD currency pair plotted with a Line Chart Style, 5 Minute Chart Period, and a Kagi Chart Study after modifying the chart settings.

Trading from the Chart

You can execute trades directly from the charting module. Tapping the Buy or Sell buttons will have two different outcomes depending on if OneTap Trading is enabled or not.

  1. If OneTap Trading is enabled, you can fill out the Volume, SL PIPS, TP PIPS, and tap Buy/Sell to instantly execute the trade. You will receive a message at the bottom of the screen confirming that the order has been processed.


  2. If OneTap Trading is disabled, you will be taken to the New Order screen. From there, you can fill out the Volume, SL PIPS, TP PIPS, Comment, and tap Buy/Sell to instantly execute the trade. You will receive a message at the bottom of the screen confirming that the order has been processed.

App Navigation

To access other modules of the application, press the icon at the top left hand corner of the screen. The current module will always display in green, in this example, our current module is the Market Watch.

Open Positions

The Open Positions module displays all open positions that you currently hold. Tapping on a specific order opens up additional options for that order.

  1. After clicking order #246, the screen expands to show further details and options for that other. From here, you can Edit the open position or opt to close it out completely.


  2. After hitting the Edit option, the Modify Position screen pops up. This screen allows you to modify the SL, TP, and the Comment. Tap Update after you are finished to complete the modification to the order. A notification will pop-up that confirms a successful modification.


  3. Tapping the close option has two different behaviors depending on if OneTap Trading is enabled or not. If OneTap Trading is enabled, the order will immediately be closed and it will display a notification that it has successfully closed. If OneTap Trading is disabled, you will be taken to the Modify Position screen – where you can hit the Close button to close the order.


Pending Orders

The Pending Orders module enables you to execute a trade based on preset conditions. Once the conditions are met, the order will execute.

a. Tapping New Order will bring up the New Pending Order dialog. The user is able to modify the Symbol, Volume, Type, At Price, SL, TP, Expiry (expiration date), and Comment. After placing the order, you will receive a notification confirming if the order was successfully placed in the pending que or not.

b. Tapping on a pending order will bring up additional options for that order. You can either Edit or Delete the pending order from the Pending Order module. Tapping Edit will open the Modify Pending Order screen for that particular order. Tapping Delete will remove the order from the que completely.

Summary


The Summary module aggregates all open positions by Symbol. This module allows you to close all positions at once based on the Symbol. In order to Close All from the Summary module, you MUST have OneTap Trading enabled (you will receive a notification to turn it on if it is not enabled).

History

The History module displays every order you have executed in WebTrader. The information displayed includes the Order ID, Symbol, Volume, Type, and Profit.

Transactions

The Transactions module displays all credits and debits to the account that is currently logged in. The information displayed includes the Transaction ID, Type, and Amount.

Settings

The Settings module allows the user to adjust several options for the WebTrader mobile application.

  1. Display Ask Line – Toggles whether or not the Ask line is displayed on the charting module for each currency pair.


  2. Display Bid Line - Toggles whether or not the Bid line is displayed on the charting module for each currency pair.


  3. Play Sounds – Toggles the sound on or off.


  4. OneTap Trading – Toggles the OneTap Trading option on or off.

Logout

The Logout option logs out your account from WebTrader and returns you to the login screen.
